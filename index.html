Karnataka Inter-Engg Colleges Champion Awarded the ‘Best Sportsman’ at College level and University level. Traveled abroad, with the Tennis teams etc. Qualified from the worlds Best Tennis Teaching Organizations ‘ United States Professional Tennis Registry’ (USPTR), South Carolina, USA and Nationally Qualified with a level-2 from All India Tennis Association (AITA), New Delhi, India. His students have performed well at National and International level Tennis Tournaments.
